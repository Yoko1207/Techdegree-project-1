Background

A few years ago, I took over my co-worker's tasks which included updating the homepage of our company. Although I was always interested in making a website, I had never written any codes before. At first, she explained to me how to change the color of a button, and let me do it on my own. I typed "red" in CSS and reload the page...BAM! The color of the button was changed to red, of course.

But that was my initial magic moment when I was hooked on coding. Instead of using a brush or colored marker, you can change color or anything else just by typing! It was a completely new concept to me.
