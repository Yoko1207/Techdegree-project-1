My Interests

Let me introduce people and things that make me happy and feel passionate about

PEOPLE

Yuzuru Hanyu

When I watched his performance for the first time, I was speechless and it seemed like time stood still. His mesmerizing fluid movement and jumps with grace and strength in perfect balance have captured my heart ever since.

Kurt Cobain

When it comes to Kurt Cobain or his band Nirvana, all I want to say is "No words needed." Kurt's distinctive and melancholic vocal with raw emotion always makes me too overwhelmed emotionally.

HOBBIES

Drawing

Ever since I can remember, I have been drawing. It's always by my side in sorrow and joy. When I draw, I feel like everything around me becomes still and silent. Just my drawing and I are active. At such a moment, all I care about is how I can express myself with pen and paper.

Reading

Along with drawing, reading is also one of my addictions. I feel uneasy if I end a day without reading. When I started to read books in English, I was so excited to feel my world was expanding. Currently, I read more English books because it's kind of expensive to buy Japanese books in the US.
